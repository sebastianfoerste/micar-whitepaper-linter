# Privacy Notes

## Data Processed

The linter may process draft white paper text, issuer descriptions, token descriptions, risk disclosures, reserve details, redemption procedures, file hashes and generated review artifacts.

## Demo Boundary

The bundled examples are fictional synthetic fixtures. Do not use real issuer names, authority correspondence, launch dates, token economics or confidential legal comments in public demos.

## Local Processing

The core linter runs locally and does not require external LLM or network processing.

## Generated Artifacts

Audit logs, remediation reports, coverage matrices, batch packs and manifests can reveal sensitive draft content or file provenance. Keep artifacts from real documents outside the repository.

## Human Review

The tool produces first-pass review artifacts. It does not certify a white paper, approve a notification or replace lawyer review.
