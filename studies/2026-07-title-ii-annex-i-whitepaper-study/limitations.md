# Limitations

This study is a deterministic first-pass review of extracted public text. It does not provide legal advice and does not determine whether any white paper satisfies MiCAR.

The sample is small and not statistically representative.

Extraction quality matters. Some PDF or rendered HTML content may not be fully captured. A disclosure may be present in the source document but absent from extracted text.

The rule set is intentionally narrow. It checks selected high-signal Annex I and Article 6 fields only. It does not check every Annex I item.

The study does not cover asset-referenced tokens or e-money tokens.

The output uses anonymized study IDs. Issuer names and raw source documents are not included in the public findings.

Human review remains required before publication of any substantive legal assessment.
